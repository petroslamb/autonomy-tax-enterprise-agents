SOC 2® - SOC for Service Organizations: Trust Services Criteria

A SOC 2 examination is a report on controls at a service organization
relevant to security, availability, processing integrity,
confidentiality, or privacy. SOC 2 reports are intended to meet the
needs of a broad range of users that need detailed information and
assurance about the controls at a service organization relevant to
security, availability, and processing integrity of the systems the
service organization uses to process users’ data and the confidentiality
and privacy of the information processed by these systems.
