A New Category in Compliance: Orchestration, Automation, and Intelligence in One Meta-Platform

Integrated Smart Compliance — the 360vue Brain, automation tools, and services in one meta-platform. Scalable, transparent, and built for digital trust.
Start Your Compliance Journey

Trusted by Switzerland’s Leading Innovation Ecosystem

The portal and brain orchestrating your compliance journey

By combining orchestration, services, and automation tools into one one unified meta-platform, built on the tradition of Swiss innovation and quality, 360vue turns compliance into what it should be: a fountain of trust and competitive advantage.

360vue introduces Integrated Smart Compliance: expert services (legal, DPO, audit) and automation tools (e.g., Drata, Secureframe) that are intelligently orchestrated inside one unified meta-platform, exemplifying Swiss innovation. At the center sits the 360vue Brain—our AI-native agentic orchestration that turns tasks and compliance evidence into a visual, adaptive plan, keeping you always audit-ready.
Unlike traditional consulting or standalone tools, 360vue is the operating layer for compliance—the foundation that scales, increases transparency, and converts compliance into your sustainable competitive advantage.

Our Service Approach: The Climb & The Core

Compliance is complex — but we’ve reimagined it to make it simple for you to execute. Our platform and services are organised into two symbiotically connected parts:

The Climb → The journey you take.

At the surface, compliance looks like a steep climb: from basecamp to summit, step by step, milestone by milestone. But with 360vue, the path is clear — every stage is mapped, orchestrated, and guided.

The climb isn’t just a journey; it’s the structure that transforms scattered tasks into a visible roadmap toward trust and certification i.e. the summit.

The Core → What powers the climb.

At the core of the mountain lies the 360vue meta-platform — where services, tools, and AI work in harmony. The 360vue Brain acts as your guide, harness, and porter, delivering automation and insight exactly when needed.

This isn’t an add-on — it’s the foundation that transforms compliance into digital trust and competitive edge.
